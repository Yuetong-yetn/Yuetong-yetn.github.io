About

My name is Yuetong Guo, currently an undergraduate student in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Fudan University, with a GPA of 3.75/4. I am currently participating in research work at Fudan University Visual Analytics and Intelligent Decision Lab (FDUVis).

My research and practice mainly revolve around visual analytics, intelligent decision-making, large language model applications, and simulation system development. I am currently continuously participating in simulation system-related projects and chart understanding research. The work I have done includes simulation kernel development and optimization, front-end and back-end development, and large model training.
